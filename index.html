About Going Through It

Being an adult is like being on a never ending roller coaster, full of ups and downs. While it can be a bumpy ride, you’re not alone. With “Going Through It”, Nat and Kenechi share their personal experiences growing up in NYC together. From relationships, sex, and breakups to finances and career advice, no topic is off-limits for this dynamic duo.

Our goal as we create this podcast is to share our experiences, struggles, and triumphs with you so that you can know you’re not alone and that we can all do this together.

We hope you'll join us for the journey!
